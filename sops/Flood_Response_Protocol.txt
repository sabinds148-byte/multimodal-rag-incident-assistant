FLOOD RESPONSE STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

1. Pre-Flood Preparation
- Drainage systems must be cleared monthly, especially during monsoon season.
- Flood-prone areas should have early warning signage and pump stations.

2. Emergency Response
- Mobile rescue units must be dispatched within 10 minutes of reported flooding.
- Barricades and road diversion signs must be deployed immediately.
- Evacuation notices must be issued via SMS and radio alerts.

3. Relief & Restoration
- Temporary shelters must be arranged for affected families.
- Water pumps should be deployed to drain flooded areas.
- Post-flood sanitization and damage inspection must begin within 24 hours.

Reviewed and enforced by the City Disaster Management Authority.
