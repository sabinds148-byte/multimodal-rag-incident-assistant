POTHOLE IDENTIFICATION & REPAIR STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

1. Identification
- Citizens may report potholes via the mobile app or hotline (dial 115).
- Road inspection drones must monitor highways weekly.

2. Assessment
- Each reported pothole must be surveyed within 24 hours.
- Risk score calculated based on depth, width, and traffic impact.

3. Repair Process
- Minor: Filled with asphalt mix and compacted by road crew.
- Major: Excavation and full resurfacing conducted by city contractor.
- Traffic redirection signage must be used during repairs.

4. Documentation
- All repairs logged in road maintenance database with photo proof.
- Monthly reports submitted to Public Works Dept.

Protocol enforced by the Roads and Infrastructure Department.
