FIRE RESPONSE STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

1. Detection & Reporting
- Any fire outbreak must be immediately reported to the city emergency hotline (dial 112).
- Reports can also be sent via the City Safety App with location and photo evidence.

2. Dispatch & Containment
- Fire & Rescue Teams must be dispatched within 5 minutes of a confirmed fire report.
- Initial containment should be attempted using portable extinguishers.
- Full-scale suppression via hydrants or fire trucks should follow.

3. Evacuation & Safety
- Residents in the affected zone should be evacuated to a nearby safe zone.
- Power and gas lines in the vicinity must be shut down.
- Emergency medical services must be alerted in parallel.

4. Investigation & Reporting
- Cause of the fire must be determined within 72 hours.
- Structural audits to be conducted if damage is structural.

This SOP must be reviewed quarterly by the Fire Department Leadership Team.
