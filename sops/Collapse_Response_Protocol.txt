STRUCTURAL COLLAPSE RESPONSE STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

1. Emergency Alert
- Any sighting of partial or full building collapse must be reported via hotline 117.
- Structural collapse suspected during earthquakes, blasts, or foundation failure.

2. Search and Rescue
- Urban Search and Rescue (USAR) teams dispatched immediately.
- Area secured and marked as hazardous; drone survey initiated if available.
- Survivors located via thermal and acoustic sensors.

3. Medical & Safety Measures
- EMS teams deployed with triage equipment.
- Gas, electricity, and water supply to affected building cut off.

4. Post-Collapse Recovery
- Structural engineers assess safety for nearby buildings.
- Site barricaded and debris removal initiated within 24 hours.
- Collapse recorded in Infrastructure Risk Register.

Reviewed quarterly by the City Infrastructure Resilience Task Force.
