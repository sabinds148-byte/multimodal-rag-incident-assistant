ELECTRICAL HAZARD RESPONSE STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

1. Hazard Identification
- Any visible spark, smoke, or burn smell near electrical infrastructure must be treated as high priority.
- Residents should immediately report via hotline or emergency app.

2. Immediate Response
- Area must be cordoned off within 10 minutes of report.
- On-call electrical technicians must isolate power supply to the affected circuit.
- Nearby transformers and breakers should be inspected.

3. Hazard Removal & Restoration
- Burned wires or faulty components must be replaced within 4 hours.
- Preventive maintenance must be conducted in the surrounding area.

4. Post-Incident Actions
- Safety audits and RCA (Root Cause Analysis) must be completed within 48 hours.
- Follow-up inspections to be logged digitally.

Maintained and approved by the City Electrical Safety Board.
