TRAFFIC ACCIDENT RESPONSE STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

1. Incident Reporting
- Traffic accidents must be reported immediately via hotline 116 or the city traffic app.
- Reports should include location, vehicle count, injury status, and any blockages.

2. Emergency Response
- Nearest traffic patrol must arrive within 10 minutes of report.
- Ambulance dispatch initiated if injuries are indicated.
- Fire department alerted in case of fuel leakage or vehicle fire risk.

3. Site Management
- Traffic rerouting initiated with signage and traffic police presence.
- Accident area photographed and documented for investigation.
- Vehicle tow team dispatched within 30 minutes to clear the scene.

4. Investigation & Logging
- Insurance and legal inspection teams notified.
- All accident data stored in central transportation incident log.

Maintained by the Urban Traffic Management & Emergency Services Department.
